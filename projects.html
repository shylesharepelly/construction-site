USHODAYA ENCLAVE

Project Type - Apartment

Discover the epitome of luxury living in the heart of Madinaguda

  • Spacious and Elegant 3BHK Layout
  • Each Floor Exclusively Dedicated to a Single Flat
  • Unmatched Privacy and Comfort
  • Prime Location in Madinaguda
  • Close to Supermarkets, Hospitals & International Schools

STRUCTURE: RCC Framed structure designed to withstand seismic activity
WALLS: Super Structure with good quality red bricks in cement mortar. 9" thick for external wall and 4 1/2" thick for internal walls and parapets.
PLASTERING: Double coat sponge finish plastering for all Internal & external surfaces with cement mortar.
DOORS: Main door with best Teak wood frame and Teak wood shutter with polishing and standard fittings. Internal doors ith teak frames(painted with synthetic enamel paint) and make flush shutters with laminate finish.
WINDOWS: UPVC sliding windows
SHELVES: Provision in Bedrooms and Kitchen
FLOORING: Double charged Vitrified title 1200mm X 600 mm (4X2) flooring of standard brand with 4" skirting in Hall, Bed rooms and Kitchen.
KITCHEN: 2' wide granite platform with stainless steel sink of standard brand & Ceramic tiles dado on walls up to a height 2' above platform.
ELECTRICALS: Main Distribution Point: power supply with copper wiring of each unit with panel board and with necessary MCBs. Living Room, Kitchen: Plug points for all the necessary lights, TV, Fans, Bedrooms & Toilets and A/c points in bedrooms, Geysers and exhaust fans points in bathrooms. Fridge, Mixer, Oven, Chimney points in kitchen shall be provided as per designs. provision for inverter connection for each flat.
PAINTING: All internal wall with putty finish & painting with premium plastic emulsion external walls with waterproof paint and texture finish at required places with asian paints. All wooden andiron surfaces are painted with synthetic enamel paint.
GENERATOR: Lift, Bore, Sump motor, Common area lighting only (2 light +2 fans)
PLUMBING: CPVC piping for all water lines and PVC pipes for all drainage pipes: Branded CP fittings and branded ceramic ware, manual lift with collapsible gates.
LIFT: 6 Passenger Manual lift with collapsible gates.
CAR PARKING: Covered car parking for each flat
SECURITY&SAFETY: CCTV Survillance for premises & common area
Note: Registration and GST charges are to be borne by the buyer. car parking, municipal water transformer, generator and other government charges should be borne by the purchaser.
